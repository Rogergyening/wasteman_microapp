About

Who we are

Our vision is to have a Green-Earth and to attain a Zero Waste status.We affirm our stance on meeting the following Sustainable Development Goals(SDG's):

  • SDG's 6 : Clean Water and Good Sanitation.
  • SDG's 14: Life Below Water.
  • SDG's 14: Life Below Water.

Our core motive is to reduce environmental pollution caused by waste through Reuse, Reduce and Recycling. With enviromental pollution being a major factor and contributor of global warming and climate, We are poised to reducing its effects and greening the earth.

  • Waste Segregation

  • Waste Pick-up

  • Recycling Of Waste

  • Awareness On Waste

Separation of waste into the various types Wastes..

We champion waste segregation from source by separating different types of waste materials, such as paper, plastic, metal, glass, and organic materials, in order to facilitate their proper disposal and reduce the negative impacts of waste on the environment.

There are several important reasons why waste segregation is important:

  • Reduces pollution
  • Increases recycling
  • Saves money
  • Promotes sustainability
  • Improves public health

Timely Pick-up of Waste

We are consistent with the regular and efficient collection of waste materials from homes, businesses, and other organizations.

Timely waste pick-up is important for several reasons:

  • Prevents accumulation of waste
  • Maintains cleanliness
  • Protects public health
  • Reduces environmental impacts
  • Enhances economic development

Timely waste pick-up requires the use of effective waste management systems, including well-coordinated collection schedules and properly functioning methods and modes of collection.

Converting waste into useful and usable products.

We pride ourselves by promoting recycling of waste by separating and collecting materials that can be recycled and processing into useful and usable products in a timely manner.

  • Environmental protection
  • Resource conservation
  • Energy conservation
  • Economic Benefit

Recycling is include collecting and processing materials that have been used or discarded, and converting them into new products. This is done in order to conserve natural resources, reduce energy usage, and reduce the amount of waste that ends up in landfills.

Creating awareness on the impact of waste

We create awareness for the society to understand and have knowledge of the impact that waste has on the environment and the importance of reducing, reusing, and recycling it.

Some of the benefits of increased awareness on waste include:

  • Environmental protection
  • Resource conservation
  • Energy conservation
  • Economic benefits

Increasing awareness on waste is important for the health of the environment and the long-term sustainability of our resources.
